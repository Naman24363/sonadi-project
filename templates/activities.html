Our Activities

Making a Difference Every Day

🐾 Animal Birth Control

Our ABC program reduces conflicts, controls dog populations, and helps eliminate rabies. A specialized unit at Nangli, Najafgarh provides surgeries, post-op care, and medical recovery for animals.

🚑 Animal Rescue

Equipped with ambulances and expert teams, we rescue and treat injured or abandoned animals. Life-saving procedures like amputations and maggot treatments are conducted daily.

🚐 Animal Ambulance Service

Our dedicated ambulance operates across Delhi, rescuing and transporting injured animals to safety. Available to the community at just a call:
{{ activity_info.ambulance_phone }} .

💉 Anti-Rabies Vaccination Drive

In collaboration with civic and veterinary groups, we run free doorstep vaccination drives to keep street dogs and cats safe and rabies-free.

🏡 Animal Rehabilitation

Fully recovered animals are released back or adopted into loving homes. We ensure follow-up checks for their continued well-being.

🐶 Adoptions and Releases

Our adoption system rehomes rescued pets. Every adoption is followed by counseling and care tips, ensuring a healthy transition.

📣 Community Education and Awareness

We conduct awareness sessions to foster compassion. Citizens are encouraged to feed, vaccinate, and care for strays with empathy and responsibility.
