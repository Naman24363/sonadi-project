Our Activities

Making a Difference Every Day

Animal Birth Control

The Animal Birth Control Program is the most effective solution for reducing conflicts between humans and animals, managing the population of street dogs, and eliminating rabies. We have a specialized and well-equipped ABC unit at Nangli, Najafgarh to perform sterilization surgeries on dogs and cats. After the surgeries, the animals are provided with special post-surgery kennels for their complete recovery, along with necessary medication.

Animal Rescue

Sonadi is well equipped with a medical ambulance facility that picks up animals from the streets that need immediate medical attention. Apart from the ambulance, we also accept individual rescuers who bring ill, injured, and abandoned animals directly to the shelter and provide further assistance to them as per their needs. Procedures such as amputations, treatment of hematoma, maggot wounds, and care for paralyzed dogs are carried out under the guidance of experts.

Animal Ambulance Service

Sonadi operates a dedicated animal ambulance service to rescue and transport injured or sick animals in need of immediate medical care. This service is available to the community, ensuring that help is just a call away. For assistance, you can reach us at +91 9212797696.

Anti-Rabies Vaccination Drive

For the past 16 years, Sonadi, in collaboration with Canis Welfare Pet Club, Indian Veterinary Organization, Delhi Municipal Corporation (DMC), and other organizations, has been running free doorstep anti-rabies vaccination facilities for street dogs and cats to protect them from rabies.

Animal Rehabilitation

After completing treatment and making a full recovery, the community animals are returned to the locations where they were picked. Every effort is made to find new homes for abandoned dogs and cats. Follow-up checks are conducted with a strict protocol to ensure that the animals have a good quality of life.

Adoptions and Releases

After completing treatment and recovery, the community animals are returned to the places from where they were picked. We make every effort to find new homes for abandoned dogs and cats. Follow-up checks with strict protocols are followed to ensure a good life for the animal.

Community Education and Awareness

At the heart of every welfare activity lies education and awareness. We encourage people to take responsibility for the dogs in their area, get them treated, vaccinated, and sterilized. We also advise feeding the strays at a fixed place to reduce dog bite cases. We encourage people to develop love and compassion towards animals, counsel them for their adoptions, and guide them on how to help animals in need.
