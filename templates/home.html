Welcome to Sonadi Charitable Trust

Founded in 2001, Sonadi Charitable Trust is dedicated to the welfare of animals and the empowerment of underprivileged communities. Our mission is to create a compassionate world where every being is treated with dignity and respect.

From providing medical care to street animals to supporting education for underprivileged children, we strive to make a positive impact in our community.
