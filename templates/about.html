Our Story

Rooted in Kindness, Growing with Purpose

Founded in 2001 under the visionary leadership of Mrs. Bandana Sengupta and Dr. Vijay Kumar, Sonadi Charitable Trust began its journey from Chittaranjan Park, New Delhi, with a single mission — to bring compassionate care to the lives of voiceless animals.

From a modest beginning, Sonadi has grown into a leading shelter and treatment facility for injured, abandoned, and mistreated street animals. With an unwavering commitment to animal welfare, our dedicated team of veterinarians, para-vets, caretakers, and volunteers has transformed thousands of lives.

Sonadi is dedicated to improving the lives of animals through various activities. These include the Animal Birth Control Program, which effectively reduces conflicts between humans and animals, manages the population of street dogs, and eliminates rabies. The organization operates a specialized and well-equipped unit for sterilization surgeries on dogs and cats, providing post-surgery care and necessary medication for their recovery.

Additionally, Sonadi conducts animal rescue operations with a medical ambulance facility, providing immediate attention to ill, injured, and abandoned animals. The organization also collaborates with various groups to run free doorstep anti-rabies vaccination drives for street dogs and cats. After receiving treatment and making a full recovery, community animals are returned to their original locations, and efforts are made to find new homes for abandoned dogs and cats. Sonadi also focuses on human education and awareness, encouraging people to take responsibility for area dogs, provide medical care, and develop love and compassion towards animals.

Over the years, Sonadi has performed more than 60,000 Animal Birth Control surgeries, treated countless animals suffering from injuries and illnesses, and provided a loving sanctuary for those who can no longer survive on the streets. Every life that enters our shelter is treated with dignity, care, and a genuine commitment to healing.

At Sonadi, we envision a future where no animal suffers alone. Our goal is to create a state-of-the-art, compassionate medical facility where abandoned and injured animals receive not only the treatment they need but also the love and respect they deserve.

Together with our supporters and the broader community, we continue to advocate for responsible animal care, promote adoption, and spread awareness about the humane treatment of animals. Join us in making the world a kinder, more inclusive place — one paw at a time.
